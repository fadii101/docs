Artnex puts a full suite of AI creative tools — image generation, video, audio, and design — in one place. This guide walks you through creating an account, navigating the dashboard, and running your first generation so you can see results before you spend a single credit.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [artnex.ai/signup](https://artnex.ai/signup) and sign up with your email address. No credit card is required to create an account.

    Once you confirm your email, you'll be logged into the dashboard with free trial credits already in your balance. These credits let you explore the tools before purchasing a pack.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    The dashboard is your home base on Artnex. From here you can access every tool category:

    - **Image** — generation, editing, upscaling, background removal, headshots, product photography, and SVG tools
    - **Video** — text-to-video, image-to-video, video extension, upscaling, face swap, and lip-sync avatars
    - **Audio** — text-to-speech and voice cloning
    - **Designer** — AI-assisted graphic design and canvas tools
    - **Templates** — pre-built starting points for common creative tasks

    Your credit balance is shown in the top navigation bar at all times, so you always know where you stand.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate your first image">
    Navigate to **Tools → Image → ImageZen** to open the all-in-one image studio.

    1. Type a description of the image you want in the prompt field. Be specific: describe the subject, style, lighting, and mood.
    2. Select a model from the dropdown. For your first generation, **Z-Image Turbo** (100 credits) is the fastest option.
    3. Choose an aspect ratio — 1:1 for square, 16:9 for landscape, or 9:16 for portrait.
    4. Click **Generate**.

    Generation typically completes in a few seconds. The result appears in the canvas area on the right.

    <Tip>
      Before you click Generate, use the **Prompt Enhancer** — Artnex's built-in tool that rewrites your prompt to be more detailed and model-optimized. It consistently produces higher-quality results, especially if you're new to writing AI prompts.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download your creation">
    Once your image is generated, you can:

    - Click **Download** to save the full-resolution file to your device (PNG or WebP)
    - Click **Edit** to open the image in the AI editor for further modifications
    - Click **Upscale** to increase the resolution using the Image Upscaler (100 credits)

    Generated images are saved to your account history, so you can come back and download them later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Free trial credits are enough to generate several images and explore the platform. When you're ready for more, pick up a [credit pack](/credits-and-pricing) — credits never expire, so there's no pressure to use them by a deadline.
</Note>
