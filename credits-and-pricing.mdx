Instead of a traditional subscription, Artnex runs on a hybrid credit system. You receive a monthly credit allowance with your plan — these reset each billing cycle and do not roll over. Need more? You can top up at any time with one-time credit packs that never expire.

## How it works

- **Subscription credits** — included with your monthly plan. Refresh every billing cycle. Unused credits do not carry over.
- **Top-up credits** — purchased separately as one-time packs. These never expire and are spent only after your monthly credits run out.

## Top-up credit packs

When your monthly credits run out, top up with a one-time pack. These credits never expire and stack with any future purchases.

| Pack | Price | Credits | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | \$10 | 10,000 | Trying the platform or occasional use |
| Creator | \$50 | 50,000 | Regular creative work and content production |
| Pro | \$100 | 100,000 | High-volume use and professional workflows |

All packs are one-time purchases. You can buy the same pack multiple times, and your balance accumulates. Payments are processed securely via Stripe.

## Estimating your usage

To give you a rough sense of what each pack covers:

- **Starter (10,000 credits)** — ~100 Z-Image Turbo images, ~41 Nano Banana images, or ~5 Veo 3 Fast videos
- **Creator (50,000 credits)** — ~500 Z-Image Turbo images, ~208 Nano Banana images, or ~25 Veo 3 Fast videos
- **Pro (100,000 credits)** — ~1,000 Z-Image Turbo images, ~416 Nano Banana images, or ~50 Veo 3 Fast videos

Real usage will vary depending on which models and tools you use.

## Checking your balance

Your credit balance is displayed in the top navigation bar on every page. You can also view a full usage history in your account profile.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do credits expire?">
    It depends on the type. **Subscription credits** are included with your monthly plan and reset each billing cycle — unused credits do not roll over. **Top-up credits** purchased separately never expire and stay in your account until you use them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I buy more credits at any time?">
    Yes. You can purchase any credit pack at any time from the [pricing page](https://artnex.ai/pricing). Credits stack — buying a second pack adds to your existing balance rather than replacing it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I run out of credits?">
    If your balance reaches zero, new generations will be paused until you top up. Your account, saved creations, and history remain fully accessible. You won't lose any of your previous work.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use credits across all tools?">
    Yes. Credits are a universal currency on Artnex. The same balance is used whether you're generating images, rendering video, creating speech, or using any other tool.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there any hidden fees?">
    No. The price on each pack is the total you pay. There are no monthly fees, no seat costs, and no feature paywalls on top of credit usage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    Yes. Artnex offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on credit packs. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of your purchase, contact the support team through the [Help page](https://artnex.ai/help) for a full refund.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use generated content commercially?">
    Yes. You have full commercial rights to everything you create with Artnex, subject to the Terms of Service.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
