Artnex gives you access to a full suite of AI image, video, and audio tools through a single account. Creating an account takes under a minute — you can sign up with your email address or link your Google account directly.

## Create an account

Go to [artnex.ai/signup](https://artnex.ai/signup) to register.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the signup page">
    Navigate to [artnex.ai/signup](https://artnex.ai/signup). You'll see the "Create Your Account" form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Enter your full name, email address, and a password. Retype your password to confirm it matches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agree to the Terms of Service">
    Check the box to confirm you've read and agree to Artnex's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Create Account**. Artnex sends a confirmation email to the address you entered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Open the confirmation email and click the link inside. Once confirmed, you're taken to your dashboard and your trial credits are ready to use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can also sign up with Google by clicking **Continue with Google** on the signup page. This skips email confirmation entirely — you'll land on your dashboard immediately after authorizing access.
</Tip>

## Log in to your account

If you already have an account, go to [artnex.ai/login](https://artnex.ai/login).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Type your email and password, then click **Sign In**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with Google (optional)">
    If you registered with Google, click **Continue with Google** instead of entering a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    After a successful login, Artnex redirects you to your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reset your password

If you've forgotten your password, you can request a reset from the login page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Visit [artnex.ai/login](https://artnex.ai/login).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot password'">
    Select the **Forgot password** link below the sign-in form. You'll be taken to [artnex.ai/forgot-password](https://artnex.ai/forgot-password).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Enter your email address and submit. Artnex sends you a password reset link. Click it to set a new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you signed up using Google, you don't have a separate Artnex password. Use **Continue with Google** to log in — password reset doesn't apply to Google-linked accounts.
</Note>

## Trial credits for new users

When you complete signup, Artnex adds trial credits to your account automatically. These credits work exactly like purchased credits — you can use them on any AI tool across the platform right away. You'll see your current balance on your dashboard and on your profile page.
