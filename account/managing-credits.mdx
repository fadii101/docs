Artnex uses a credit system instead of a subscription. You buy a pack of credits once and spend them as you generate — images, videos, audio, and more. There are no monthly fees and no expiration dates. When you need more, you top up.

## How credits work

Every AI tool on Artnex has a credit cost. When you run a generation, the cost is deducted from your balance. Costs vary depending on the model and output type — lightweight image tools cost far fewer credits than high-end video models.

Every tool shows its credit cost before you generate, so you always know what you'll spend. As a rough guide, $10 buys you 10,000 credits, which works out to roughly **$0.001 per credit**.

<Note>
  Credits are not a subscription. You pay once and use your balance until it runs out. There are no automatic renewals.
</Note>

## Check your credit balance

Your current balance appears in two places:

- **Dashboard** — shown as a badge near the top of the page when you're logged in.
- **Profile page** — go to `/profile` and look for the Credits badge in your profile header.

## How to buy credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the pricing page">
    Visit [artnex.ai/pricing](https://artnex.ai/pricing) or click **Buy Credits** from your profile or dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a pack">
    Select the Starter, Creator, or Pro Pack based on how many credits you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in if prompted">
    If you're not logged in, Artnex redirects you to the login page first, then returns you to pricing after authentication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Stripe checkout">
    You're taken to a secure Stripe checkout page. Enter your payment details and confirm the purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credits are added instantly">
    After a successful payment, Artnex verifies the transaction and adds your credits immediately. You'll see a confirmation message and your updated balance on your profile page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  After a successful purchase, Artnex redirects you to `/profile?payment=success`. If your balance doesn't update within a few seconds, refresh the page. If credits still aren't showing after a minute, contact support — do not complete the checkout a second time.
</Tip>

## What happens when you run out of credits

If your balance reaches zero, Artnex blocks new generations. You'll see an error when you try to run a tool. To continue, purchase a credit pack and your balance will be topped up immediately.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="Do credits expire?">
  No. Credits you purchase never expire. You can buy a pack today and use the credits months later — there's no time limit.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I get a refund on unused credits?">
  Credits are one-time purchases and are generally non-refundable once bought. If you believe you were charged in error or experienced a technical issue during checkout, contact Artnex support with your payment details and session ID.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use credits across all tools?">
  Yes. Credits are shared across your entire account and work with every AI tool on the platform — image generation, video, audio, image editing, and processing tools all draw from the same balance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my payment information stored by Artnex?">
  No. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. Artnex never stores your card details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if my payment went through but credits weren't added?">
  Go to your profile page (`/profile`). If the credits aren't showing, wait up to one minute for the verification to complete, then refresh. If the problem persists, contact support with your Stripe session ID — do not attempt to purchase again.
</Accordion>
