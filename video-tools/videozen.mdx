VideoZen is Artnex's main AI video studio. You can generate videos from a text prompt alone, or bring an image to life by animating it into a full video clip. With over 11 models from Google, OpenAI, Alibaba, Kling, and ByteDance, you have the right tool for every creative brief — from quick social content to cinematic sequences.

## Modes

VideoZen offers two generation modes, selectable at the top of the prompt bar.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Text-to-video">
    Write a prompt describing the scene, motion, and style you want. VideoZen sends it to the selected model and returns a video clip. No source image is needed.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Image-to-video">
    Upload a starting image (and optionally an ending image for supported models) to animate it into video. Your prompt guides the motion and continuation of the scene.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Settings

You can adjust the following settings before generating a video. Available options vary by model.

| Setting | Options | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Aspect ratio | 16:9, 9:16, 1:1 | 1:1 is available on Kling and WAN models |
| Duration | 4–12 seconds | Default is 5s; exact options depend on the model |
| Resolution | 720p, 1080p | Some models also support 480p |

## Generate a text-to-video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open VideoZen">
    Navigate to **Tools > Video > VideoZen** in the Artnex sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Text-to-Video mode">
    At the top of the prompt bar, click **Text-to-Video** to make sure you are in the right mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your prompt">
    In the text area, describe the video you want to create. Be specific about the subject, setting, camera movement, lighting, and mood for best results.

    <Tip>
      Use the Prompt Enhancer (the sparkle icon in the prompt bar) to automatically expand and improve your prompt before generation. This works especially well for short or vague descriptions.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a model">
    Click the model selector button in the control bar. A dialog lists all available models grouped by provider. Select the one that fits your quality and budget needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust settings">
    Use the aspect ratio, duration, and resolution controls to match the output format you need — for example, 9:16 for vertical short-form content or 16:9 for widescreen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate**. The credit cost for the selected model is shown next to the button before you confirm. Generation typically takes 30 seconds to 3 minutes depending on the model and duration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your video">
    Once the video appears in your creations gallery, click the **Download** icon to save it to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate an image-to-video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Switch to Image-to-Video mode">
    Click **Image-to-Video** in the mode selector at the top of the prompt bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a start image">
    Click **Add Image** (or **Start**) in the control bar to upload the image you want to animate. The image becomes the first frame of the video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optionally upload an end image">
    For models that support it — including Seedance v1.5 Pro, Kling v2.5 Turbo Pro I2V, and Veo 3.1 I2V — you can also upload an end frame. The model generates the motion that connects the two frames.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a motion prompt">
    Describe how you want the image to move or evolve. For example: *"The camera slowly zooms in while the flowers sway gently in the wind."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a model and settings">
    Choose an image-to-video capable model from the model selector. Adjust aspect ratio, duration, and resolution to match your intended output.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate** to start the process. Credits are charged once the video is successfully produced.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prompt Enhancer

The Prompt Enhancer is available as a sparkle icon inside the prompt bar. When you click it, Artnex rewrites your prompt with richer descriptive language, camera direction, and cinematic detail before sending it to the model.

<Note>
  The Prompt Enhancer replaces the text in the prompt field with the enhanced version. Review the result before generating so you can adjust anything that doesn't match your vision.
</Note>

## Downloading videos

Generated videos appear in your **My Creations** gallery on the VideoZen page. To download a video:

1. Hover over the video thumbnail in the gallery.
2. Click the **Download** icon.
3. The file saves to your default downloads folder.

<Tip>
  All videos are saved to your Artnex Creations library and remain accessible from any device while you are signed in.
</Tip>
