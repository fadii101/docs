Templates are pre-built AI creative effects that let you transform photos in seconds. Instead of configuring AI settings from scratch, you pick a template, upload your photo, and Artnex handles the rest. Templates cover everything from artistic style transfers to cinematic video animations.

## Navigate to Templates

Go to **Tools → Templates** in the left sidebar. The Templates gallery opens, showing all available effects organised by category.

---

## Browse by category

Use the category tabs along the top of the gallery to filter templates by type. Click **Discover** to see all categories at once, or **Trending** to sort by the most-used templates.

## How to use a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Templates">
    Go to **Tools → Templates** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a template">
    Browse the gallery or use the category tabs to find the effect you want. Click a template card to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your photo">
    On the template page, click the upload area and select the photo you want to transform from your computer. Some templates require two photos (for example, Face Swap asks for a source face and a target image).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate**. Artnex runs the AI effect and displays the result. The credit cost is shown on the template page before you start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or continue editing">
    Once the result is ready, download it directly or open it in the Canvas editor for further adjustments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Credits

Each template consumes a different number of credits depending on how computationally intensive the AI model is.

<Note>
The credit cost for each template is displayed on the template page before you click Generate. Video templates generally cost more credits than image templates.
</Note>

<Tip>
Start with one of the high-usage templates like **Ghibli Style** or **Image Face Swap** to get a feel for how templates work before using credits on heavier video effects.
</Tip>
