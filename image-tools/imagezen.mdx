ImageZen is the core image creation tool in Artnex. It brings together text-to-image generation, image-to-image editing, prompt enhancement, and image-to-prompt extraction — all in one interface. Whether you're creating from scratch or transforming an existing photo, ImageZen gives you a full model lineup to match your quality and speed needs.

## Navigate to ImageZen

Go to **Tools > Image > ImageZen** in the Artnex sidebar.

## Available models

ImageZen groups its models into three tiers: Fast, Pro, and Edit. Select a model by clicking the model button in the bottom control bar, then choosing from the dialog.

## Generate an image

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open ImageZen">
    Navigate to **Tools > Image > ImageZen**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a prompt">
    Type your description in the prompt field at the bottom of the screen. Be specific: include subject, style, lighting, and mood.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a model">
    Click the model button in the control bar to open the model picker. Choose the model that fits your quality and credit budget.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set aspect ratio">
    Use the aspect ratio selector next to the model button. Options vary by model — most support 1:1, 16:9, 9:16, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create">
    Click **Create** (with the credit cost displayed) to start generation. A progress indicator appears while your image is being generated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or save">
    When the image appears in your creations gallery, hover over it to reveal the download button. All generated images are automatically saved to your Creations library.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use image-to-image (edit mode)

Edit models let you upload a reference image and transform it using a text prompt.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an Edit model">
    Open the model picker and choose any model from the **Edit Models** group. The interface updates to edit mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload reference images">
    Click **Upload** in the control bar and select up to 10 images (PNG, JPEG, or WebP). Thumbnails appear in the prompt bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the edit">
    Write a prompt describing the change you want — for example, "change the background to a snowy forest" or "make the lighting more dramatic."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Click **Edit** to run the generation. The edited image is saved to your Creations gallery automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>Edit models require at least one uploaded image. The **Edit** button stays disabled until you upload a reference.</Warning>

## Prompt Enhancer

The Prompt Enhancer rewrites your prompt to be more detailed and model-optimized, without changing your core idea.

To use it in ImageZen:

1. Type your prompt in the prompt field.
2. Click the **sparkle icon** (✦) at the bottom-right of the prompt field.
3. Artnex replaces your prompt with the enhanced version automatically.

You can also use the standalone **Prompt Enhancer** tool at **Tools > Prompt Enhancer** for more control, including context, detail level, and tone settings.

## Image-to-Prompt

Image-to-Prompt analyzes an uploaded image and generates a descriptive prompt you can use — or modify — for generation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Image-to-Prompt dialog">
    Click the **image icon** at the bottom-right of the prompt field to open the Image-to-Prompt dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload an image">
    Drop or select any image file. Artnex analyzes it and returns a detailed prompt describing the scene.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply or edit the prompt">
    Click **Apply** to load the generated prompt into the prompt field, then edit it as needed before generating.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading and saving creations

All generated images are saved automatically to your Creations gallery, accessible from the ImageZen canvas or the global **Creations** section.

To download an image:
- Hover over any image in your gallery and click the **download icon**.
- The file downloads in the format you selected at generation time (JPEG, PNG, or WebP depending on the model).

## Tips for better prompts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Be specific about style" icon="palette">
    Instead of "a woman," write "a close-up portrait of a woman in soft natural light, editorial style, shallow depth of field." Specific style cues produce more consistent results.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Describe lighting" icon="sun">
    Lighting has one of the biggest impacts on image quality. Include terms like "soft studio lighting," "golden hour," "dramatic side lighting," or "overcast diffused light."
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use the Prompt Enhancer" icon="sparkles">
    If you're unsure how to phrase your idea, type a short description and click the sparkle button. The enhancer adds relevant technical details for you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Iterate with seeds" icon="refresh-cw">
    When using Z-Image Turbo, set a seed to lock in a composition you like, then vary only the prompt. Use -1 to get a new random result each time.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>For portrait or social media content, 4:5 is often a better fit than 1:1 — it fills more of a phone screen and matches Instagram's recommended format.</Tip>
